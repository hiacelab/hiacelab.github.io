Dr. Nade Liang is the director of the Human-in-the-loop Advanced Cognitive Engineering (HiACE) Lab at Texas Tech University. His research focuses on human factors, cognitive engineering, human-automation interaction, and autonomous driving systems, with an emphasis on improving safety, usability, and human-centered system design.
