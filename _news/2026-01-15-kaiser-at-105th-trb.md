**Kaiser Hamid** presented his research at the **105th Transportation Research Board (TRB) Annual Meeting**, held in **Washington, D.C., January 11–15, 2026**.

The presentation featured his study titled **“Driver Visual Attention Modeling via Vision-Language In-Context Learning,”** conducted under the supervision of **Dr. Nade Liang**.

The work was presented during a poster session and drew interest from researchers and practitioners in **transportation safety, human-centered AI, and autonomous driving**.

The TRB Annual Meeting is one of the world’s largest international conferences in transportation research, bringing together experts from academia, industry, and government to discuss advances in transportation systems and safety.
