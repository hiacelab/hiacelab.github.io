HiACE Lab PhD student **Kaiser Hamid**, supervised by **Dr. Nade Liang**, was selected to present his research titled:

**“A New Evaluation Metric for Takeover Maneuver Quality: Comparing Human Drivers with Autonomous Driving Agents.”**

The presentation was scheduled for the **69th Human Factors and Ergonomics Society (HFES) International Annual Meeting**, held **October 13–17, 2025**, at the **Hyatt Regency Chicago**.

This study introduces a new evaluation metric for **takeover maneuver quality** during transitions in conditionally automated driving. By comparing the behavioral responses of **human drivers** and **autonomous driving agents**, the work assesses safety, smoothness, and control stability in time-critical scenarios.

The proposed framework contributes to more standardized performance measures for evaluating **human–automation collaboration** and improving the design of takeover intervention systems.
