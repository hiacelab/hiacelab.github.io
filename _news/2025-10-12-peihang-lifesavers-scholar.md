HiACE Lab PhD student **Peihang Li** was selected as a **2025 Lifesavers Traffic Safety Scholar** and attended the **Lifesavers Conference on Roadway Safety**, held **March 9–11, 2025**, in **Long Beach, California**.

The national program recognizes undergraduate and graduate students who demonstrate commitment to improving **traffic safety through research and practice**.

Peihang was one of **29 students across the United States** selected through a competitive process to engage with leading experts in engineering, public health, law enforcement, and transportation policy. The scholarship provided support for conference participation and travel expenses.
