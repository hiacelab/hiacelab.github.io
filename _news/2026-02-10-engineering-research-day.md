At **Texas Tech University Engineering Research Day**, the HiACE Lab was represented by two PhD students, **Kaiser Hamid** and **Peihang Li**, who presented their latest work.

Kaiser shared research on the **computer vision and perception** side, focusing on how learning-based systems can interpret visual data for robust scene understanding in real-world environments.

Peihang presented research on the **human factors** side, studying human behavior, perception, and interaction with intelligent systems, with an emphasis on improving safety and human-centered design.

Together, their presentations reflect the broader direction of the HiACE Lab: advancing intelligent systems that are both technically strong and human-aware.
